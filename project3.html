UX Process

1

User Research

  • Studied target audience (businesses, organizations seeking green solutions).
  • Defined user needs: easy access to resources, credibility, clear visuals.

2

User Flow & Wireframe

  • Structured navigation: Home – Services – Resources – Race to Zero – News.
  • Focused on logical grouping and consistency.

3

Visual Design

Low-fidelity mockups, refined into clean, minimal, eco-themed visuals.

4

Prototype & Testing

Tested clarity of information and CTA visibility across devices.
